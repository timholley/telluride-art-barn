About the Art Barn

The Art Barn is a truly stunning as well as incredibly unique property nestled on San Juan Mesa, Colorado, surrounded by the LaSalle Mountains. Minimalist, with clean lines, light wood and floor-to-ceiling windows, it is the perfect blend of western cool and modern aestheticism. About 40 minutes from Telluride and 25 minutes from Ridgway, the Art Barn is the ideal place to get away from it all and enjoy nature. Inviting decks let you see as far as Utah, and overlook a sloping meadow that alternates between glittering snow, colorful summer wildflowers and striking fall hues of gold and orange.

A true, tranquil paradise, elk and deer roam by in herds, the quiet nearly complete—the grass really is greener here. All this, along with a state of the art 1000-square-foot, fully equipped artists studio make it the ultimate getaway for writers, artists, musicians or anyone who just wants to relax. The studio was designed with artists and sculptors in mind—its floor-to-ceiling doors can be opened fully to move large objects—but it can easily be transformed into two additional bedrooms.
